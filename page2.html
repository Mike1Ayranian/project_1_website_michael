Fun Facts about Daft Punk!

  • Thomas Bangalter wears the helmet that looks a little bit like a letter-box, while Guy-Manuel wears the smoother helmet.
  • One time, David Bowie came to the studio of Daft Punk and asked the duo if they could remix one of his songs. The duo politely declined.
  • In the 2008 Grammy's, Daft Punk made a surprise appearence. The duo appeared with Kanye West to perform a newer version of one of Kanye's songs, "Stronger", and making the duo's first ever televised live performance.
  • In 2003, the band created an animated feature length movie called "Interstella 5555: The 5tory of the 5ecret 5tar 5ystem" to accompany their album "Discovery". Several scenes from the movie were used to make short music videos to accompany the music in the album "Discovery".
  • In 2006, Daft Punk created a movie called "Electroma." The movie was about the two robots trying to become human. This was their first project that did not feature any of their music, and the entire movie did not have any dialogue.
  • To accompany the album "Discovery," Daft Punk made helmets that had LED lights in the helmets that could produce animated words, phrases, and faces that would show outside of the helmets. The first of these helmets that were created for the duo cost about $65,000 to produce and create.
  • "Get Lucky" (Ft. Pharell Williams) was a single that they released in their newest album "Random Access Memories." This single had sold over 7 million copies worldwide and had won the duo three Grammy Awards in 2014.
